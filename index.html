About Us

WRONG SIZE started with a simple idea: clothes shouldn't fit expectations — they should fit you. What began as a late-night sketch on a torn notebook turned into a bold movement.

Born in a small room, inspired by oversized hand-me-downs and the freedom of not fitting in, WRONG SIZE was created to challenge the fashion system. We design loose-fit streetwear that embraces imperfection — oversized, undersized, asymmetrical, raw.

Every piece represents rebellion, authenticity, and individuality. Our style is rooted in street culture, shaped by real people and real stories. WRONG SIZE isn’t just a brand — it’s a mindset. For those who refuse to blend in, for those who wear confidence louder than clothes. This is your fit, your way — even if it's the wrong size.
